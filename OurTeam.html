Engineers, Architector, CTO

Stefan Tschirren

Started his professional career almost 20 years ago by joining a National engineering consultancy in a division focusing in light gauge steel design and shop detailing of framed construction in the residential, commercial, and light industrial sectors. Holding both technical and managerial roles within the business, Stefan was involved in overseeing major projects, systems development, providing engineering solutions to internal and external clients, innovative design projects, research and development, mentoring staff, strategic planning, resource management, and general expansion of the division’s client base. With on-ground experience in several states across Australia, and design projects that cross international borders, Stefan is able to provide effective design solutions to projects of many scales. In 2018 Stefan co- founded STS Structural and leads the team as the Principal Structural Engineer. Stefan is the QLD Chairman of The National Association of Steel-Framed Housing, is active with the Australian Steel Institute, and has assisted on Australian Standards committees. You may catch Stefan presenting technical lectures at conferences around Australia, online, or via various eLearning portals.

Matthew Tomkins

Matt joined the STS Structural team in 2021. With over 15 years of experience in the Built Environment industry, Matt has gained considerable knowledge in various structural engineering sectors from architectural housing, volume housing, industrial complexes, commercial re-purposing projects, asset assessments and forensic investigations. Matt has gained doctoral qualifications (PhD) and has taught as a sessional lecturer (Bond University) in the field of structural engineering for construction management courses. Matt is a curious and committed engineer who is passionate about the possibilities of transdisciplinary design in the Built Environment.
